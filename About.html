Soft Swerve Ice Cream is the newest addition to the Lower East Side. The owners are Michael T, and Jason L. Both are very passionate about ice cream and the area. So much, that they had decided to name al of the items on their "Specials" menu after popular locations aroung New York City. Soft Swerve specializes in fine ice cream with four different favorite ice cream flavors to choose from. With just a couple weeks into their opening, their most popular flavor has been the Ube flavor, which is also one of their favorite! Another popular popular is the Black Sesame, which is a recommendation if you are planning to stop by.
